IKEA Review Collector

About This Page

Puzzled over the lack of buyer reviews on the Singapore IKEA website? Browsing in IKEA when something catches your eye, but you are hesitant to purchase it over concerns of the product's quality? Never fear - this small project allows you to capitalise on some of the collective wisdom of Ikea buyers from around the world!

How To Use:

Simply type a product name to search for on the IKEA SG site. The search results will be presented and you can choose a product from there to collate reviews for. Due to technical limitations, results may take some time to collate and display.

Search for product here:

Search terms should be as specific as possible (omitting product dimensions) e.g. "NORDLI bed frame" instead of "NORDLI".
